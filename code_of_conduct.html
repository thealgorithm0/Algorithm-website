The Algorithm Community - Code of Conduct

Code of Conduct

Introduction

The Algorithm is dedicated to empowering women in technology. We aim to create an inclusive, supportive, and respectful community where everyone feels welcome. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all members and participants in our community.

Community Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome women of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences. By women we mean sis, trans and biological.
  • Respect: Treat others with respect and kindness. Discrimination, harassment, or exclusionary behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Empowerment: Support and uplift each other, fostering sisterhooedand an environment where everyone can grow and succeed.
  • Collaboration: Encourage collaboration and knowledge sharing. Everyone has something valuable to contribute.
  • Integrity: Act with honesty and integrity in all interactions.

Expected Behavior

  • Respectful Communication: Engage in respectful, constructive, and inclusive discussions.
  • Be Considerate: Consider how your actions and words affect others. Refrain from making assumptions about others' backgrounds, abilities, or experiences.
  • Supportive Environment: Offer help and support to others, particularly those who are new or less experienced.
  • Acknowledge Contributions: Recognize and value the contributions of others. Give credit where it's due.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment: Harassment of any kind, including but not limited to, offensive comments, deliberate intimidation, stalking, or unwelcome attention.
  • Discrimination: Discrimination based on gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, religion, or any other personal characteristic.
  • Disruptive Behavior: Disruption of events, talks, or other activities. This includes inappropriate use of online platforms and resources.
  • Violating Privacy: Sharing private information of others without their consent.

Consequences of Unacceptable Behavior

To set clear expectations, here is what will happen if someone breaks this Code of Conduct:

  • Warning: For minor violations, the person will receive a formal warning explaining why their behavior was inappropriate and what is expected of them in the future.
  • Temporary Ban: For more serious or repeated violations, the person may be temporarily banned from community activities and events.
  • Permanent Expulsion: In cases of severe or continuous violations, the person may be permanently expelled from the community. This decision is made to ensure the safety and well-being of all community members.

Reporting and Enforcement

  • Reporting Incidents: If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the community leaders at thealgorithm0@gmail.com.

    Investigation: All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

    Action: Appropriate actions will be taken based on the severity and frequency of the behavior, following the consequences outlined above.

Commitment to Improvement

  • The Algorithm is committed to continuously improving our community standards. We encourage feedback and suggestions from all members to help us create a safer and more inclusive environment.
